About Us

Handsome Hipster Coffee Roasters was born out of necessity. The necessity of having truly good tasting organic coffee. We got tired of drinking the trash Starbucks serves and decided that we could do it better. Owners, Quinoa and Lincoln, left their high paying corporate jobs, and sold everything they had to open their own coffee roasting business. Handsome Hipster is proudly located in the heart of Portland, OR.

We care so much about the quality of our coffee that we take extra care to hand roast in small batches. We also care about the environment. Our warehouse was built only using reused sustainable materials. All of our employees are required to use only organic products and eat a vegan diet. Every time we visit one of our coffee farms, we plan a new tree. Giving back to our Earth and community is our highest priority.
